Detailed Research Summary

Max Planck Institute (Germany)

Focus Projects
  • No-collapse accurate measurement-based feedback control.
  • Quantum parameter estimation via continuous observation.
  • Non-Markovian feedback for optimized QEC.
  • RL for quantum computing experiments.
Key Contributions

Introduced protocols for precisely estimating noise-free signals and full density matrices for feedback control, significantly improving RL training efficiency.

Developed a recurrent neural network approach for measurement-based QEC that builds an internal representation of the quantum state, extending logical qubit lifetimes beyond current best protocols.

OIST (Japan)

Focus Projects
  • Phonon-assisted carrier cooling in graphene.
  • Measurement-Based Feedback with RL.
  • Estimator scheme for continuous QEC.
  • Open-loop control for motional cooling.
Key Contributions

Demonstrated that RL can discover real-time controls for complex systems undergoing continuous measurement, specifically for preparing non-classical cat states.

Proposed a QEC method using real-time estimators that exceeds the break-even point for small codes.
