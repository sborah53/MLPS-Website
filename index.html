Current Research Interests

  • ML for Quantum Technology: Developing machine learning methods to tackle computational and design challenges across quantum technologies.
  • Quantum Control: Designing robust quantum control protocols using AI for high-fidelity state preparation and control operations.
  • Quantum Error Correction: Creating data-driven quantum error-correcting schemes to stabilize fragile quantum information.
  • Quantum ML: Exploring variational circuits and kernel-based models for quantum-enhanced learning tasks.
  • Neuromorphic Computing: Building brain-inspired architectures that harness quantum and neuromorphic principles for efficient computation.
  • ML for Photonics: Using ML to inversely design and optimize complex photonic structures and devices.
  • ML for Condensed Matter Physics: Leveraging ML to classify phases, discover emergent behavior, and accelerate simulations in condensed matter systems.
  • Applications of ML and Quantum Technology for fundamental interdisciplinary research in physics, chemistry, material science and other domains.
